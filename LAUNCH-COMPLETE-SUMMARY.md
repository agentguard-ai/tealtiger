The industry's first offline AI agent security guardrails:

1. **PIIDetectionGuardrail**
   - Detects: emails, phones, SSNs, credit cards
   - Actions: block, redact, mask, allow
   - Risk scoring: 0-100

   - Configurable sensitivity levels
