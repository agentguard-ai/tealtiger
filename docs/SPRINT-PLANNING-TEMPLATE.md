## Template Usage Instructions

1. **Copy this template** for each sprint planning session
2. **Fill in sprint information** at the beginning of planning
3. **Use during planning meeting** to guide discussion and capture decisions
4. **Save completed template** in `docs/sprints/sprint-[number]-plan.md`
5. **Reference during sprint** for daily standups and progress tracking
