## Overview

This document defines the **scope of TealTiger v1.1.0**.

v1.1.0 is a **foundational governance and control release**, focused on establishing **deterministic policy enforcement**, **auditability**, and **cost‑aware decisioning** for AI and agent workloads.

The goal of this version is **trust, clarity, and stability** — not full autonomy or advanced orchestration.

---

## Design Intent of v1.1.0

TealTiger v1.1.0 is designed to:

- Establish a **stable policy and governance core**
- Enable **security, cost, and risk visibility**
- Support **monitor and enforce** policy modes
- Be **SDK‑first** and developer‑friendly
- Provide **enterprise‑grade audit foundations**

This version intentionally avoids complex runtime orchestration or autonomous agent control.

---

## In‑Scope Capabilities (v1.1.0)

### 1. Policy Evaluation Framework

Included:
- Deterministic policy evaluation
- Condition → action execution model
- Logical composition of conditions (`AND`, `OR`, `NOT`)
- Policy modes:
  - `monitor`
  - `enforce`

Policies can evaluate **security**, **cost**, **risk**, and **governance** signals.

---

### 2. Conditions and Actions

Included:
- Input, model, cost, risk, and execution context conditions
- Enforcement actions:
  - `allow`
  - `restrict`
  - `block`
- Transformation actions:
  - `redact`
  - `mask`
- Cost control actions:
  - token limits
  - model switching
- Observability actions:
  - logging
  - metrics emission

Actions are ordered, auditable, and policy‑driven.

---

### 3. Risk Scoring (v1.1.0)

Included:
- Normalized risk scores (`0–100`)
- Domain‑level risk signals:
  - security
  - cost
  - governance
  - reliability
- Composite risk score calculation
- Policy threshold evaluation on risk scores

Risk scoring is deterministic and versioned.

---

### 4. Reason Codes

Included:
- Structured, machine‑readable reason codes
- Domain‑based taxonomy:
  - SECURITY
  - COST
  - RISK
  - GOVERNANCE
  - RELIABILITY
- Multiple reason codes per decision
- Stable identifiers for audit and automation

Reason codes explain *why* a decision was made.

---

### 5. Cost Metadata

Included:
- Token usage metadata (input/output/total)
