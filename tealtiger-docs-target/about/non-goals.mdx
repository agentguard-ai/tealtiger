> **Audience:** Developers, Security Architects, Platform Owners

## Purpose

This document clearly states what **TealTiger is intentionally not designed to do** in the current release.  
Defining non‑goals is critical to:

- Prevent misuse of the SDK
- Avoid overlapping responsibilities with other platforms
- Keep TealTiger focused, predictable, and composable
- Enable safe evolution across future versions (v1.2.x, v1.3.x)

---

## Non‑Goals in v1.1.0

### 1. TealTiger is **not a model hosting or inference platform**

TealTiger does **not**:
- Host LLMs, ML models, or agent runtimes
- Replace OpenAI, Anthropic, Bedrock, Vertex AI, or self‑hosted inference stacks
- Manage GPU scheduling, batching, or inference optimization

TealTiger operates **around** AI systems, not **inside** them.

---

### 2. TealTiger is **not a prompt engineering or prompt optimization tool**

TealTiger does **not**:
- Generate, refine, or optimize prompts
- Suggest better prompt templates
- Perform automatic prompt rewriting or tuning

Prompt design remains a **developer responsibility**.  
TealTiger only **evaluates and governs** prompt behavior when policies are applied.

---

### 3. TealTiger is **not a replacement for IAM, KMS, or Secrets Managers**

TealTiger does **not**:
- Manage user authentication or authorization
- Store API keys, secrets, or credentials
- Replace IAM systems (AWS IAM, Azure AD, GCP IAM)
- Replace KMS or secret vaults

TealTiger **consumes identity context**, it does not own identity.

---

### 4. TealTiger is **not a SIEM or log analytics platform**

TealTiger does **not**:
- Provide dashboards for long‑term log analysis
- Correlate events across infrastructure sources
- Replace SIEM, XDR, or SOAR platforms

Instead:
- TealTiger emits **structured audit events**
- Events are designed to be forwarded to SIEM/log platforms

---

### 5. TealTiger is **not a data loss prevention (DLP) engine**

TealTiger does **not**:
- Classify or discover sensitive data at rest
- Perform deep content inspection across enterprise datasets
- Replace enterprise DLP products

TealTiger focuses on **AI interaction governance**, not enterprise data discovery.

---

### 6. TealTiger is **not a real‑time incident response or blocking system**

In v1.1.0, TealTiger does **not**:
- Automatically quarantine agents or workloads
- Trigger automated incident response playbooks
- Perform kill‑switch actions across infrastructure

Policy enforcement is **deterministic and scoped**, not reactive incident handling.

---

### 7. TealTiger is **not an AI ethics or fairness evaluation framework**

TealTiger does **not**:
- Evaluate model bias, fairness, or societal impact
- Certify models for ethical compliance
- Perform human‑value alignment assessments

TealTiger enforces **technical governance**, not ethical judgments.

---

### 8. TealTiger is **not a business cost optimization or FinOps platform**

TealTiger does **not**:
- Perform budget forecasting or chargeback
- Optimize vendor pricing or contracts
- Replace FinOps tooling

Cost governance in v1.1.0 is:
- **Policy‑driven**
- **Contextual**
- **Focused on AI usage behavior**, not finance operations

---

### 9. TealTiger is **not a UI‑first or dashboard‑centric product**

TealTiger does **not**:
- Rely on heavy UI workflows
- Require dashboards for core functionality
- Enforce UI‑based configuration

TealTiger is **SDK‑first**, **API‑driven**, and **infrastructure‑friendly**.

---

### 10. TealTiger is **not designed to make security decisions opaque**

TealTiger does **not**:
- Use black‑box or probabilistic enforcement
- Hide why a policy passed or failed
- Apply undocumented heuristics

All decisions are:
- Policy‑defined
- Deterministic
- Auditable

---

## Intentional Design Philosophy

TealTiger is intentionally **narrow but deep**.

It focuses on:
- AI policy enforcement
- Cost, security, and reliability governance
- Deterministic, explainable decisions
- Developer‑native integration

Anything outside this scope is **explicitly left to adjacent systems**.

---

## Forward‑Looking Note

Some non‑goals in v1.1.0 **may evolve** in future versions:

- v1.2.x: Expanded governance controls
- v1.3.x: Platform‑level intelligence and coordination

However, TealTiger will **never become a monolithic AI platform**.

---

## Summary

If you are looking for:
- AI governance ✅
- Cost, security, and reliability controls ✅
- Deterministic enforcement ✅

TealTiger is the right tool.

If you are looking for:
- Model hosting
- Prompt generation
- SIEM dashboards
- FinOps forecasting

TealTiger intentionally stays out of that space.
