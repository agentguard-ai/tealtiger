## Purpose

This document helps teams quickly answer a simple question:

> **“Is TealTiger the right tool for us?”**

Clarity here prevents misalignment, failed pilots, and incorrect expectations.

---

## TealTiger Is For You If…

### ✅ You build or operate AI‑powered systems

TealTiger is designed for teams working with:
- LLMs
- Agentic AI systems
- AI‑driven workflows
- ML inference pipelines with governance needs

---

### ✅ You need deterministic governance

TealTiger is a good fit if you care about:
- Predictable enforcement
- Explainable decisions
- Reproducible outcomes
- Audit‑grade evidence

If “it behaved differently this time” is unacceptable — TealTiger fits.

---

### ✅ You want policy‑based control over AI behavior

TealTiger is for teams that want to govern:
- AI cost usage
- Security exposure
- Reliability constraints

…using **explicit, versioned policies**, not heuristics or dashboards.

---

### ✅ You prefer SDK‑first, infrastructure‑friendly tools

TealTiger works best for teams that:
- Integrate governance directly into code
- Use CI/CD and GitOps workflows
- Avoid UI‑only or click‑driven enforcement

---

### ✅ You already have adjacent systems — and want TealTiger to complement them

TealTiger integrates *around*:
- IAM
- SIEM
- Observability platforms
- Model providers

It does not attempt to replace them.

---

## TealTiger Is NOT For You If…

### ❌ You want an AI model hosting or inference platform

TealTiger does not:
- Host models
- Run inference
- Manage GPUs
- Optimize inference latency

---

### ❌ You expect probabilistic or “AI‑driven” enforcement

TealTiger will not:
- Learn policies automatically
- Make fuzzy or adaptive decisions
- Change behavior without policy updates

Determinism is non‑negotiable.

---

### ❌ You are looking for a SIEM, FinOps, or DLP replacement

TealTiger does not replace:
- SIEM dashboards
- FinOps forecasting tools
- Enterprise DLP platforms

It emits governance signals — it does not analyze everything.

---

### ❌ You want a UI‑first governance experience

If your organization expects:
- Point‑and‑click configuration
- Heavy dashboards
- Minimal code involvement

TealTiger may not be a good cultural fit.

---

### ❌ You want governance without engineering discipline

TealTiger assumes:
- Versioned policies
- Reviewed changes
- Intentional governance

If governance is treated as an afterthought, results will disappoint.

---

## Summary

TealTiger is ideal for teams that want:

- Explicit AI governance
- Deterministic enforcement
- Developer‑native integration
- Strong auditability

TealTiger is intentionally **not** for teams seeking:
- Black‑box enforcement
- UI‑only control
- Model hosting or optimization
